01 — Brand Foundation

Who We Are

Betti is an intelligent senior care platform that connects residents, caregivers, emergency services, and facility operators through a unified ecosystem of real-time dashboards, health monitoring tools, and smart communications. Our brand reflects our commitment to trust, clarity, and compassionate technology.

Mission

To empower senior living communities with intelligent, real-time tools that improve quality of life, accelerate emergency response, and reduce caregiver burden.

Vision

A world where every senior lives with dignity, safety, and connection — supported by technology that works quietly in the background.

Core Values

Care First. Every feature begins with the resident. Technology is a means, never the end.

Brand Personality

Warm but professional. Calm under pressure. Precise but human. Trustworthy above all else.

06 — Usage Guidelines

Do's & Don'ts

Consistent brand application builds recognition and trust. Follow these guidelines across all communications — digital, print, and presentation.

✓ Do

  • Use Betti Navy (#233E7D) as the dominant brand color in headers and navigation
  • Use Betti Green (#5C7F39) for calls-to-action, positive indicators, and highlights
  • Maintain sufficient contrast — minimum 4.5:1 ratio for all body text
  • Use the approved logo files provided by the brand team
  • Keep UI language calm, clear, and compassionate
  • Use Arial/Segoe UI at defined weights for digital interfaces
  • Apply proper clear space around the Betti logo at all times

✗ Don't

  • Don't alter the logo colors, proportions, or add effects
  • Don't use brand colors at low opacity in ways that reduce legibility
  • Don't use all-caps in body copy — it reads as aggressive in care contexts
  • Don't combine both brand fonts in the same layout element
  • Don't place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a clear backdrop
  • Don't use red or high-saturation colors for general UI — reserve for critical alerts only
  • Don't reproduce the brand materials without authorization from Providence Wave Group

Voice & Tone

Betti communicates with the warmth of a trusted caregiver and the clarity of a skilled clinician. Language should always be simple, direct, and reassuring. Avoid jargon. Never alarm without context. In emergency scenarios, prioritize accuracy and brevity over style.
